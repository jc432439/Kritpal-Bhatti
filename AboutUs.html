About Us

We are a family-run business based at Hulkstown and were established in the year 2012. We deal in creating custom-made costumes for our clients, from a simple costume like - spiderman to a more complex one with additional features like armour, animatronics, realistic weaponry etc. Depending upon the complexity of the design the dress may take between 1 to 50 weeks in making and costs between as low as $10 to $10,000. We also Buy and sell second hand costumes!!!!!
